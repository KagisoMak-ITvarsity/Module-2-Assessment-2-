About Pine City Mall

Pine City Mall is the premier shopping destination in the heart of the city. With over 100 stores, restaurants, and entertainment options, we offer an unparalleled shopping experience for the whole family.

Since opening our doors in 2010, we've been committed to providing our visitors with world-class retail, dining, and entertainment options in a beautiful, comfortable environment.

Our spacious food court features a wide variety of cuisines from around the world, offering something delicious for every taste. Enjoy your meal in our modern, comfortable seating area with natural lighting.
